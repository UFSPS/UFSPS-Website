The easiest way in is to connect and show up.

If you are interested in signal processing, technical workshops, or chapter projects, start with the public channels below. From there, you can track upcoming events and find the best way to participate.

Step 1

Join the communication channels

Discord and Instagram are the fastest paths to announcements, reminders, and general chapter visibility.

Step 2

Come to an event

The best way to understand the chapter is to attend a workshop, talk, or project night and meet the people there.

Step 3

Stay close to the work

Use the project and resource pages to find the part of the chapter that aligns with your interests.

What to expect

A chapter that takes technical growth seriously.

Members should expect programming that is clear, technically grounded, and designed to leave them with a sharper understanding than they came in with.
